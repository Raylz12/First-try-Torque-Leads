## QUICK TIPS

### Subject Lines That Work:
- Keep them short (5 words max)
- Use their name or shop name
- Ask a question
- Create curiosity
- Avoid spam triggers ("FREE", "ACT NOW", etc.)

### Body Best Practices:
- Keep it under 150 words
- One clear CTA (book a call)
- Write like a human, not a marketer
- Use the $16M shop credential early
- Mobile-friendly formatting (short paragraphs)

### Timing:
- Best days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- Best times: 7-8 AM or 5-6 PM (before/after shop hours)
- Avoid Mondays and Fridays

### Tracking:
- Track opens and clicks
- Log responses
- Note which subject lines perform best
- Follow up within 24 hours of any response
