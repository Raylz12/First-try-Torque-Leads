# TorqueLeads Cold Call Script v2
*Straight talk for shop owners*

---

## OPENER

**Gatekeeper:**
> "Hey, I need to talk to [OWNER]. It's about getting more trucks in his shop."

**Owner picks up:**
> "Hey [NAME], I'll be quick — you got empty bays right now?"

*(If yes — keep going. If no — "Good for you. Call me when that changes." and hang up.)*

---

## THE HOOK (Pick one that fits)

**The Money Hook:**
> "Every day one of your bays sits empty, that's a grand you're not making. I can fix that."

**The Competition Hook:**
> "There's 50 trucking companies within 30 miles of your shop. How many of them know you exist? I can put your shop in front of all of them."

**The No-BS Hook:**
> "I ran a diesel shop for 12 years. Did $16 mil. I'm not some marketing kid who's never turned a wrench. I know what works."

**The Pain Hook:**
> "Tired of waiting for the phone to ring? Word of mouth's great until it dries up. Let me show you something better."

**The Direct Hook:**
> "I get fleet trucks into diesel shops. That's it. You want more work or not?"

---

## QUICK PITCH (30 seconds max)

> "Here's the deal. I run ads on Facebook and Instagram that target every trucking company, fleet manager, and owner-operator near your shop. Not Google crap. Not SEO. Actual ads that put your shop in their face every day until they call you."

> "Most of my shops see 5-10 new fleet leads in the first month. One good fleet account pays for a year of this."

---

## THE CLOSE

**Hard close:**
> "Look, I've got 15 minutes tomorrow at 2. I'll show you exactly how it works, no pitch, no contract talk. You in or not?"

**Softer close:**
> "Give me 15 minutes. If it's not for you, I'll never call again. But if it is — you could have trucks lined up by next month."

**Walk-away close:**
> "I'm only taking on 3 new shops this month. You want one of those spots or should I call the guy down the street?"

---

## OBJECTIONS — KEEP IT SHORT

**"Not interested"**
> "You got full bays? ... No? Then you're interested, you just don't know it yet. 15 minutes."

**"I don't do marketing"**
> "Good. Neither do I. I get trucks in your bays. That's it. 15 minutes."

**"Send me info"**
> "Info doesn't pay your rent. A conversation might. Tomorrow, 2pm. Yes or no?"

**"How much?"**
> "Depends on your area. But I'll tell you this — one fleet account covers a whole year. Let's talk numbers tomorrow."

**"I've been burned before"**
> "By some SEO guy who promised page one and delivered nothing? Yeah, I've heard that story 100 times. This ain't that. 15 minutes and I'll prove it."

---

## VOICEMAIL (20 seconds)

> "Hey [NAME], it's [YOUR NAME]. I put trucks in diesel shops. Got a list of 50 fleets near you that don't have a shop yet. Call me back at [NUMBER] if you want first crack at them. [NUMBER]. Later."

---

## RULES

1. **Don't apologize for calling.** You're offering money, not asking for it.
2. **Keep it under 2 minutes.** They're busy. Respect that.
3. **Don't oversell.** Just get the meeting.
4. **If they say no twice, move on.** There's another shop to call.
5. **Sound like a guy, not a salesman.** No scripts voice. Just talk.
