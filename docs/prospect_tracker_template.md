# TorqueLeads Prospect Tracker

## Status Codes
- **NEW** — Just added, not contacted
- **CALLED** — Left voicemail or talked briefly
- **EMAILED** — Sent first email
- **FOLLOW-UP** — In follow-up sequence
- **BOOKED** — Call scheduled
- **PITCHED** — Had discovery call
- **WON** — Signed up
- **LOST** — Said no
- **DQ** — Disqualified (wrong fit, out of business, etc.)

## Tracking Fields

| Shop Name | Owner | Phone | Email | City | Status | Last Contact | Next Action | Notes |

| Example Diesel | John | 555-1234 | john@example.com | Dallas | CALLED | 3/5 | Follow-up call 3/8 | Busy, call back |

## Weekly Goals
- **Calls:** 50/week (10/day)
- **Emails:** 100/week (20/day)
- **Booked calls:** 5/week
- **Closed deals:** 1-2/week

## Conversion Benchmarks
- Call → Booked: 10-15%
- Email → Reply: 5-10%
- Booked → Pitched: 80%
- Pitched → Won: 30-40%

## Follow-up Schedule
- Day 0: Initial call + email
- Day 3: Follow-up email
- Day 5: Follow-up call
- Day 8: Break-up email
- Day 30: Re-engagement (if no response)

## Notes
- Best call times: 7-8 AM, 5-6 PM (before/after shop hours)
- Best email times: Tuesday-Thursday
- Always log every contact attempt
- Move to DQ if no response after 30 days
